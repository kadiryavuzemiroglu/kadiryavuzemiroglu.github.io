Case Studies

Solsten × Prominent Casual Card Game Company | 5 weeks

Scoping the Future of a Classic

One of the largest mobile card game companies in the world needed to understand their competitive landscape and options to grow further. Trying to understand how an already-big player can innovate with minimized risk, I conducted foundational research to understand the competitive landscape, player motivations, and potential market opportunities.

Work Done

  • Market research to align on main competitors
  • Expert review and feature breakdown
  • Interview players of each competitor based on themes and differentiators

Outcome

Insights revealed key opportunities to differentiate the product through personalized challenges, flexible monetization, and social features. A surprising insight inspired a nostalgic campaign that successfully attracted players from competitors.

Solsten | B2B SaaS · Self-serve Data Platform | Continuous (12+ months)

Driving Product Clarity

With vast data, the product held immense potential, but how to dynamically prioritize development and drive clarity? I led continuous research efforts with ideal customer profile (ICP) users, primarily marketing and audience strategy leaders, to surface key use cases and uncover what drives conversion.

Work Done

  • Continuous discovery research, wireframe testing, user interviews, use case definition, user insights workshop
  • Building & monitoring product analytics alongside user interviews
  • Organizing periodical user insights workshops to evangelize user data and collect product team's questions

Impact

Helped the team prioritize features for a product-led growth (PLG) model and define clearer paths to value in a complex product environment. Grounded product decisions in both qualitative and quantitative insights.
